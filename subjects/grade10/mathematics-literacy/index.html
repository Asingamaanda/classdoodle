What you will learn

Term 1 focuses on everyday maths: interpreting numbers, money calculations, measurement in real situations, reading plans and maps, and basic data handling.

Numbers in Context

  • Percentages, ratios, and rates.
  • Use of units and correct notation.

Worked Examples

Example 1: Discount

A jacket costs R450. It is discounted by 20%. Find the new price.

Show solution

Discount = $0.20 \times 450 = 90$

New price = $450 - 90 = \text{R}360$

Example 2: Simple Interest

Invest R2 500 at 6% per year for 3 years. Find interest earned.

Show solution

$I = Prt = 2500 \times 0.06 \times 3 = \text{R}450$

Practice Questions

  1. A grocery bill is R684 with 15% VAT included. Find the VAT amount.
  2. Convert 2.4 km to meters and 850 m to kilometers.
  3. A map scale is 1:50 000. What real distance is 6 cm on the map?
  4. Calculate the mean of: 12, 15, 10, 8, 15.
  5. A taxi charges R12 flag drop plus R6 per km. Write a formula for cost and find cost for 9 km.
